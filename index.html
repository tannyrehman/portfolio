Automatic usage-based volume discounts

Workspacify automatically applies the following tiered volume discounts for total monthly usage that exceeds 100,000 minutes:
Minimam used Discount level
100,000 to 499,999 5%
500,000 to 999,999 7%
1,000,000 to 3,000,000 10%

These discounts apply to the usage within each tier. For example, if Workspacify bills an account for 600,000 minutes, the usage from 1 to 99,999 minutes receives no discount, the usage from 100,000 to 499,999 minutes receives a 5% discount, and the usage from 500,000 to 600,000 minutes receives a 7% discount.
